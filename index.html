About me

Hey there! I'm Andreas, but you can call me Andy. I'm a graphic design student from and based in Copenhagen, Denmark. I'm 21 years old and want to be a full‑time graphic designer.

I “suffer” from AuDHD and anxiety—it's been hard, but I’ve learned to adapt and use it as a strength.

I study Graphic Design at NEXT Education Copenhagen, currently at NEXT's School Education Center in Herlev. Here, I'm searching for brands to find an apprenticeship at and work with.

My whole life I've loved playing around in Photoshop, editing videos in Premiere Pro, and being creative in general. In my free time I’m a gamer, tech lover, and an all‑around nerd. I’m inclusive, social, and love building connections.
