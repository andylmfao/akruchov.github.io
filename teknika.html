Project details

At Teknika, I was responsible for creating a variety of design materials to support the school's branding and communication efforts. This included designing posters for events, creating marketing material for digital and physical posters, and crafting social media templates to maintain a consistent online presence. I helped ensure that all designs aligned with Teknika's brand identity and effectively conveyed key messages to our target demographics.

  • Goals: awareness, clarity, consistency
  • Audiences: students, staff, visitors
  • Output: digital + print production
